Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a grievance?
To submit a grievance, click on the "Create New Post" button from the homepage or use the sidebar icon. Fill out the required information including title, category, description, and location. You can also attach relevant documents or images to support your grievance.
How long does it take to get a response?
Response times vary depending on the nature and urgency of the grievance. Typically, you should receive an initial acknowledgment within 24-48 hours, and a more detailed response within 5-7 working days.
Can I track the status of my grievance?
Yes, you can track your grievance status by logging into your account and visiting the "My Posts" section. Each grievance will show its current status and any updates from the authorities.
What types of issues can I report?
You can report various civic issues including but not limited to:
  • Infrastructure problems (roads, bridges, buildings)
  • Sanitation issues
  • Public safety concerns
  • Educational facility issues
  • Healthcare-related concerns
  • Environmental problems
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and only used for communication regarding your grievances. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.
Can I submit anonymous grievances?
While you can submit anonymous grievances, we recommend creating an account and identifying yourself to receive updates and responses to your submissions. This also helps in following up on your grievances effectively.
